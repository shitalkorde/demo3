Tally

  • Tally is an ERP accounting software package that is used to record day to day business data of a company.
  • The latest version of Tally is Tally ERP 9.
  • Tally ERP 9 Software is one of the most financial accounting systems used in India.
  • For small and medium enterprises, it is complete enterprise software.
